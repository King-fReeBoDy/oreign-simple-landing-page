who we are

joy light graphic design inc is a wholly-owned Ghanaian company. The company has been in active operations since the year 2003 providing quality and affordable commercial, digital and advanced screen printing services to individuals, public and private organisations. With our professional experience, the company has undeniably been identified amongst leading print houses for the provision of corporate promotional and publicity events items in Ghana. We are recognised experts in designing and printing corporate annual reports, brochures, calendars, magazines, and value books. We also undertake the printing and embroidery of Polo and T-shirts, Mugs, Pens, Keyrings, Baseball Caps, Car Branding, Flexi Banners/ Vinyl materials and many other business souvenirs. We are a full-fledged printing house that provides you with end-to-end high-quality printing solutions. We let your thoughts and emotions take shape, colour and designs that are etched forever in one's memories.
